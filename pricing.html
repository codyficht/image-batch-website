Pricing Tiers

Discover the Right Tier for Your Needs

We offer various pricing tiers for Image-Batch to cater to different user requirements. Each tier offers unique capabilities to handle images.

To upgrade to Pro or Premium, simply click the 'Upgrade' button at the bottom of the app and follow the prompts. Enhance your Image-Batch experience effortlessly!

Lite Version (FREE):

This is the basic version of Image-Batch, letting you access its foundational features.

  • Process up to 3 images at once.
  • Bulk resize (by width), reduce/compress, crop, convert (JPEG only), watermark, rename, and find duplicates.

Pro Upgrade ($4.99 USD):

Step up your editing game with extended features, as marked by the icon on our home/features page.

  • Process up to 40 images at once.
  • Enter custom output folder names.
  • Direct Edit Mode: Modify images in their original folder.
  • Extend conversion options to PNG, HEIF, and TIFF.
  • Choose custom crop and watermark positions.
  • Resize by width or height
  • And all the core features from Lite!

Premium Upgrade ($12.99 USD):

Experience the full potential of Image-Batch with our most comprehensive features, as highlighted by the icon on our home/features page.

  • Process an unlimited number of images at once. Tested with over 500 images!
  • Reduce/Compress to a specific desired file size per image.
  • Precision cropping by exact dimensions.
  • Exclusive WEBP conversion access.
  • Plus, all the benefits of Pro!



Note: Existing customers who made a one-time purchase will continue to have unlimited access to all features, along with free updates.
