Pricing Tiers

Lite Version (FREE):

This is the basic version of Image-Batch, letting you access its foundational features.

  • Process up to 3 images at once.
  • Bulk resize (by width), reduce/compress, crop, convert (JPEG only), watermark, rename, and find duplicates.

Pro Upgrade ($11.99 USD):

Step up your editing game with extended features for heavy workflows.

  • Process up to 40 images at once.
  • Enter custom output folder names.
  • Direct Edit Mode: Modify images in their original folder.
  • Extend conversion options to PNG, HEIF, and TIFF.
  • Choose custom crop and watermark positions.
  • Resize by width or height.
  • Direct image selection.
  • Access to the settings menu.
  • And all the core features from Lite!

Premium Upgrade ($24.99 USD) Most Popular

Experience the full potential of Image-Batch with our most comprehensive features for unlimited power.

  • Precision cropping by exact dimensions.
  • Advanced, largest side resizing.
  • Exclusive next-gen AVIF and WEBP conversion access.
  • Access to the rotate features.
  • Plus, all the benefits of Pro!
